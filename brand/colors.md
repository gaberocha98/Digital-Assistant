# Digital Assistant — Brand Colors

## Primary Palette
| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary | Teal | #0D9488 | Headers, section titles, progress bars |
| Primary Dark | Deep Teal | #115E59 | Dark backgrounds, header bars |
| Primary Light | Mint | #CCFBF1 | Light cell backgrounds, alternating rows |
| Accent | Amber Gold | #F59E0B | Highlights, CTAs, "left to spend" indicators |
| Accent Light | Soft Gold | #FEF3C7 | Light accent backgrounds |
